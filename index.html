About

I am a researcher and engineer with a background that spans three distinct but complementary disciplines. Currently pursuing an M.Sc. in International Material Flow Management at Trier University of Applied Sciences in Germany, while working as a Research Assistant at the Institute for Applied Material Flow Management (IfaS) on EU-funded sustainability programmes.

My career began in analytical chemistry at the Industrial Technology Institute in Colombo — precision work, accredited analysis, ISO compliance. That foundation informed my move into software quality assurance at Axiata Digital Labs, where I automated test suites for mobile and web platforms across Malaysia and Sri Lanka.

Since moving to Germany, I have channelled both disciplines into environmental research: building LCA tools for agri-food SMEs, conducting GHG accounting, and developing the digital infrastructure that makes EU-funded science visible and accessible.

I build things that are measurable, verifiable, and impactful.
